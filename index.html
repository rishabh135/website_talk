About the Talk

Manifold Mixup is an extension of popular paper Mixup ( A new learning principle which trains a neural network on convex combinations of pairs of examples and their labels) encourages the network to produce more reasonable and less confident predictions at points with combinations of attributes not seen in the training set.

This is accomplished by training on convex combinations of the hidden state representations of data samples. Using this method, the authors demonstrate the improved semi-supervised learning, learning with limited labeled data, and robustness to adversarial examples.
